For this example, three more pages have been created to navigate between using the footer at the bottom of the page. There's this page (displayed as home), Messages, Settings, and Donate.

The pages don't do anything apart from show dummy content, allowing you to interact with the footer properly.

This type of footer consists of a ≤menu> element with multiple <li> elements, each with their own anchor.

The currently-selected li can be given a class name of "selected" by your server/client side framework to highlight it as the currently-selected tab.
